STRUCTURAL DESIGN
LAYER 01

The Data Aggregation Engine

The foundation of the GAIN OS relies on un-siloed, asymmetric data collection. The Behavioural Processor

Raw data is passed through a psychological and political filter. The architecture maps detractor friction, economic buyer motivations, and shadow hierarchies to construct the definitive account reality. It's not about what the client says—it's about how the board buys.

GOVERNANCE & OPEN SOURCE
RISK MITIGATION

AI Hallucination Governance

LLMs invent when unconstrained. Our governance protocol restricts generative output strictly to validated inputs. The architecture mandates verifiable sourcing for all financial claims, completely neutralizing boardroom liability and eliminating the Execution Gap.

Open Source Philosophy

Proprietary black-box models create vendor lock-in and decay over time. GAIN leverages open-source LLM interoperability, allowing the OS to swap models seamlessly as the frontier advances. We ensure persistent, state-of-the-art analytical superiority without platform dependency.
