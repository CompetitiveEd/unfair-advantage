WHERE THIS BREAKS

FAILURE 1: The Fake Coalition

You had 3 "Yes" votes, but you didn't check if they got along. The CIO hated the CFO's plan. Your proposal forced them to align, so they killed it to avoid the conflict.

RULE: Don't just map individuals. Map the friction between them.

FAILURE 2: The Hostile Sponsor

Your champion was loud and supportive. You didn't realise they were losing a power struggle internally. Their support actually became a weapon used by rivals to block the deal.

RULE: Verify your champion's political capital, not just their job title.

FAILURE 3: The Wrong Map

You mapped the Global HQ perfectly. But the decision was actually delegated to the Regional MD. You spent 6 weeks influencing people who had no vote.

RULE: Confirm governance delegation for this specific contract value.
