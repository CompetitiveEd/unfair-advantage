03. THE 90-DAY ARC

Every transformation started the same way. Not with a strategy deck. With a diagnostic. Pick one deal. Apply the system. Win it. The person sitting next to the winner asks 'how did you do that?' — and adoption begins.

DAYS 1-30

GATHER + ASSESS

Intelligence, not action.

Financial baseline — the real P&L, not the reported one. Account tiering — top 10, contenders, whitespace. Identify the unstructured cost — the invisible waste that's been there so long nobody notices it.

"The diagnostic doesn't find surprises. It finds what everyone knows but nobody has written down."

DAYS 31-60

INFLUENCE + BUILD

Pick the fight.

Value proposition sharpened to one sentence. Nine customer evidence stories any seller can deploy. Then: pick one strategic complex deal. Deploy full GAIN methodology. Arm the champion.

"If a seller can't explain the framework in two minutes, they won't use it."

DAYS 61-90

WIN + MEASURE

Evidence, not celebration.

The strategic deal closes. Pipeline coverage is measurably improved. Win rate is tracked. The team sees it work on a real deal with real money. Then: plan the next cycle.

"That's how adoption works — not by mandate, by evidence."

WHY THIS WORKS
