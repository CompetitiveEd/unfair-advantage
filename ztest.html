  • The AI Starts Repeating Itself: You ask for a battle card and the output looks suspiciously similar to something it generated three prompts ago.

  • The Output Gets Generic: Early outputs were specific and detailed. Later outputs sound like they could apply to any company in any industry.
  • Hallucination Increases: Facts start appearing that weren't in any source material you provided.
