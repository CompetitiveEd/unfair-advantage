05. TACTICAL UTILITIES
  • 01
    THE COMPUTE DIVIDE

    Free LLMs produce quick results, not good results. They suffer from 'Lost in the Middle' syndrome on long documents and lack the reasoning depth for complex politics.

    Free tiers (GPT-4o mini / Gemini Flash): Baseline indications only.
    Reasoning models (Opus 4.6 / Gemini 2.5 Pro / GPT-4o): Where true execution lives. If the stakes are high, use models that think, not just predict.

  • 02
    THE AUTOMATION FALLACY

    If you automate the entire deal into a single 'Mega-Prompt', you automate the curiosity right out of the room. You lose the "Wait a minute, that's interesting..." moment that leads to the true differentiator. The prompts are a chessboard, not a conveyor belt. Run them in sequence, review the output, and steer the next move.

  • 03
    READ CRITICALLY, NOT GRATEFULLY.

    AI produces confident, well-structured text regardless of accuracy. Your job is to verify the hard facts and filter the synthetic hallucinations before the briefing.

  • 04
    SANITISE THE INPUT.

    Never upload proprietary pricing or unannounced PII into public AI models. Use abstractions. Data governance is the price of admission for enterprise AI execution.
