The "Boring But Expensive" Budget Finding Strategy

How to win deals competitors don't even see by identifying problems with budget already allocated.

The Insight: Boring = Budget

Everyone wants to sell "innovation." But board-visible, "unsexy" problems like integration complexity and manual reporting often have faster decision cycles and less competition.

The Six "Boring" Categories:

  • Integration Challenges: Manual data reconciliation.
  • Manual Processes: Spreadsheet-based workflows.
  • Compliance Burdens: Audit risk and labor.
  • Technical Debt: End-of-life software support.
  • Vendor Lock-In: Licensing premiums.
  • Data Silos: Fragmented inconsistent data.

The Discovery Reframe

Don't ask "Do you have a problem?" Ask "How much are integration workarounds costing you quarterly in labor?". Benchmarking makes it safe for them to admit the cost.
