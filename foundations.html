01. The 2026 Market Pattern

Marc Sumner (Robertson Sumner) identified that the rarest and most in-demand roles are those blending technical literacy with commercial acumen. In 2026, AI fluency is the baseline; human judgement is the moat.

The McKinsey Ownership Rule

Elite firms now screen for individuals who "maintain ownership of decisions when AI outputs are imperfect." Success is orchestration, not just prompt engineering.

02. The Management Gap

High-value Managed Services deals are found in the distance between what the Board promises the market and the internal friction hindering that promise. This is the Management Gap.
