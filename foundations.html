The 2026 Market Pattern

Marc Sumner (Robertson Sumner) identified that the rarest and most in-demand roles are those blending technical literacy with commercial acumen and soft skills. In 2026, AI fluency is the baseline; human judgement is the differentiator.

The McKinsey Ownership Rule

Elite firms now screen for individuals who "maintain ownership of decisions when AI outputs are imperfect". Leadership has shifted from managing human teams to orchestrating **human-agent workflows**.

The Principle: The **Accusation Audit**. Surface their project anxieties before they do to prove you understand their world.
