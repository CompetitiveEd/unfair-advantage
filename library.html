01. The 2026 Market Pattern

Marc Sumner (Robertson Sumner) identified that the rarest and most in-demand roles are those blending technical literacy with commercial acumen. In 2026, AI fluency is the baseline; human judgement is the differentiator.

The McKinsey Ownership Rule

Elite firms now screen for individuals who "maintain ownership of decisions when AI outputs are imperfect." Success is orchestration, not just execution.

02. The Strategic Hierarchy

Companies do not buy products; they buy to implement initiatives that support their strategies and goals. Most sales teams stop at the departmental level. We start at the Board level.

The Management Gap

High-value deals exist in the distance between what the CEO promises the market and the internal friction hindering that promise.
