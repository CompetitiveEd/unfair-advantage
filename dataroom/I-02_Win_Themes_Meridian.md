# I-02: WIN THEME ARCHITECT — Meridian Financial Services

## SCQA
**SITUATION:** Meridian is tendering a £115M Managed Services contract. Evaluation weighted toward Technical (60%) and Commercial (30%) with heavy focus on transition risk and gain-share outcomes.

**COMPLICATION:** Competitors will pitch "low-risk continuity" (Tesseract) or "scale" (Global SIs). Helios risks being perceived as too small or too "transparent."

**ANSWER:** The single most powerful narrative is "The Regulatory Rescue: Structural Resilience without the Change Trauma."

## THE UNDENIABLE SHIFT
"The world has changed because the PRA now treats Operational Resilience as a personal liability for Board members (SS1/21). The old approach of 'Managed Services as a black box' was built for low accountability; Helios's approach was built for a world where only Audit-Grade Infrastructure Reality protects your license to operate."

## THE CATEGORY FRAME
- **DEFAULT:** IT Outsourcer (Body Shop)
- **TARGET:** FS Regulatory & Resilience Partner
- **REFRAME:** "We are not a technology outsourcer; we are a regulatory remediation partner that happens to manage your technology."

For Jennifer Morris to say to the CRO/Audit: "Helios is the only bidder that doesn't view 'Resilience' as a technical checkbox; they view it as a regulatory mandate. Rachel Okonkwo has actually done this for the PRA's peers — they give us the data we need to clear our Section 166 warning on Day 1."
