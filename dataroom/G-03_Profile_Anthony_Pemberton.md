## Meridian Financial Services

## SCQA
**SITUATION:** Sir Anthony Pemberton is the Senior Independent Director and Chair of the Board Audit & Risk Committee. He is a corporate governance specialist, chartered accountant (FCA), former Deputy Governor & CEO of the PRA. Knighted in 2019 for services to British Banking. 40-year unbroken career spanning the collapses of Arthur Andersen and the 2008 financial crisis.

**COMPLICATION:** He is personally embarrassed and reportedly "furious" that Meridian has received private regulatory warnings from the PRA — the exact agency he used to run. He views "transformation" buzzwords as dangerous fluff and has weaponized his authority to commission an independent EY audit, deliberately freezing the CIO's cloud budget until he gets "Governance Certainty."

**ANSWER:** Sir Anthony does not buy technology, speed, or innovation; he buys regulatory armor and risk elimination to protect his personal legacy.
