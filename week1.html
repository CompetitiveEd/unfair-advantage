Tactical Intel Workflows

Calibrate your research based on the stakes of the meeting. Not every lead requires an 8-pillar deep dive.

Level 1: Quick Intelligence (20 min)

Gather the annual report, recent press releases, and executive posts. Identify top 3 priorities and repeating language patterns.

Level 2: Person-Level Intelligence (45 min)

Analyze the stakeholder's career trajectory and decision-making style. Search LinkedIn Jobs for hiring signals that indicate budget and timeline.

Level 3+: Enterprise Deep Dive (90-120 min)

The 8-Pillar Protocol: A high-stakes pursuit framework covering foundations, financials, tech strategy, and infrastructure.

The Executive Briefing Template

Don't walk into a meeting with raw notes. Use this structured layout to present your intelligence internally or as a professional proposal.

Target Profile

  • Role: Position & Summary
  • Bio: Expertise & Achievements
  • Company: Model & Offerings

Meeting Context

  • Source: Referral/Pathway
  • Pain Points: Identified Challenges
  • Objective: Primary Goals

Personal Profile

  • Interests: Hobbies & Community
  • Messaging: 3-4 Core Value Props
  • Proof: Relevant Case Studies
