Pre-mortem + go/no-go verdict
DEAL VERDICT PROCEED WITH CONDITIONS — Win probability: 62% Condition 1: Run G-03 on Richard Hale (CIO) before any direct engagement. CIO tension with Morris is the deal's fault line.
