Tom had 30 minutes before a CFO meeting. He ran the annual report through the G-01 research prompt. He found the CFO used "cost-to-serve" 17 times and "innovation" twice.

THE PSYCHOLOGY (SAFETY)

Research is not about gathering facts. It is about lowering buyer cortisol. Humans are biologically wired to fear vendor failure. When you ask basic discovery questions ("Tell me about your priorities"), you trigger that fear. When you quote their own earnings call back to them to frame your solution, you prove you are a safe pair of hands.
