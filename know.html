THE NARRATIVE

Tom had 30 minutes before a CFO meeting. He ran the annual report through the G-01 research prompt. He found the CFO used "cost-to-serve" 17 times and "innovation" twice.

He dropped "digital transformation" from his pitch and led with "operational efficiency." The CFO leaned forward and said: "You're the only one who seemed to understand what we're actually trying to do."

THE PSYCHOLOGY (SAFETY)

Research is not about gathering facts. It is about lowering buyer cortisol. Humans are biologically wired to fear vendor failure. When you ask basic discovery questions ("Tell me about your priorities"), you trigger that fear. When you quote their own earnings call back to them to frame your solution, you prove you are a safe pair of hands.

THE ENGINE LOGIC

This is not a standard prompt; it‚Äôs an IF/THEN routing engine. It reads your three variables and autonomously decides whether to build a full strategic sales pitch, conduct an unbiased deep-dive into a specific domain, or audit a single company.

THE POWER OF "NONE"

Don't force a sales pitch if you're just doing early discovery. By typing 'None' into the Seller Company or Focus Area fields, you instruct the AI to drop its 'sales bias' and act strictly as an objective, deep-research intelligence analyst.

ROUTING EXAMPLES

Full Strike: Target + Seller + Focus = Tailored pitch.
Domain Recon: Target + 'None' + Focus = Unbiased intelligence.
Seller Audit: 'None' + Seller + 'None' = Own proposition breakdown.

DEFINING THE DOMAIN

The AI is only as sharp as the boundaries you set. If your Focus Area is just 'IT', you will get generic rubbish. Be highly specific. Use terms like 'End User Computing & Service Desk Consolidation' to hunt for high-value pain points.

TARGET COMPANY: [Insert Target, e.g., Rolls-Royce plc, or type "None"]

## ROLE & OBJECTIVE

## EXECUTION ROUTING LOGIC (READ CAREFULLY)
You must analyze the three variables above and choose the correct execution path. Do not deviate from the assigned path.

* PATH 1: THE FULL STRIKE (Target + Seller + Focus are all provided)
  * Action: Execute all phases. Research the Target generally, research the Target specifically in the Focus Area, research the Seller, and write a targeted pitch mapping the Seller to the Target's Focus Area pain points.
* PATH 2: DOMAIN RECONNAISSANCE (Target + Focus are provided | Seller is "None")

* PATH 4: GENERAL ACCOUNT PLAN (Target + Seller are provided | Focus is "None")

3. COMPLIANCE BURDEN: Audit preparation labour, certification maintenance.
4. VENDOR DEPENDENCY: Over-reliance on single vendors creating premium pricing.
5. TECHNICAL DEBT: End-of-life systems, extended support contracts.

For each finding:
‚Äî Estimate the annual cost where data supports it
‚Äî Identify who owns this budget (which department/function)
