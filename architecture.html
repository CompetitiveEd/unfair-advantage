The Operating Doctrine

AI is just a retrieval engine. Without a structural framework, it only retrieves noise. These are the established principles governing every prompt.

01. Behavioural Science — Decoding the Buyer
[I]
Loss Aversion
Kahneman & Tversky

Humans feel loss twice as intensely as gain. They default to the status quo to avoid perceived risk.

GAIN: Win themes must neutralise the fear of transition before promising a future gain.
[I]
Psychology of Persuasion
Cialdini

Decisions accelerated by cognitive shortcuts: Social Proof, Authority, Scarcity, Liking, Consistency, Reciprocity.

GAIN: AI scans for exact peer-industry matches to trigger Social Proof in executive summaries.

Known Limitations
01
Source Quality

Feed it marketing collateral instead of verified 10-Ks and raw transcripts, the outputs will be generic and useless.

Countermeasure: Strict input validation at every prompt node.

02
Human Layer

The engine maps influence perfectly, but a human still has to walk into the room, hold eye contact, and execute.

Countermeasure: Behavioural science coaching built into the methodology.

03
Compute Discipline

Unconstrained LLM usage burns API budget rapidly. Targeted, conditional prompts — not open-ended queries.

Countermeasure: Tiered access with prompt governance per role.

04
Intelligence Decay

Outputs lose their edge if source feeds aren't refreshed quarterly. Stale transcripts mean stale politics.

Countermeasure: Automated refresh triggers on earnings cycle.
