The Shift

AI made every pitch perfect. Perfect is now table stakes. The only edge left is judgment — reading the room, decoding the politics, knowing when to walk away. GAIN is the operating system that turns that judgment into a repeatable, scalable architecture. It does in 70 minutes what used to take 40 hours of pulling filings, scanning Glassdoor, cross-referencing LinkedIn, and staring at spreadsheets. You used to do all of this manually. Now you don't have to.

But it's not autopilot. The AI surfaces intelligence. You decide what it means. You spot the thing the system can't see — the relationship your mate told you about, the fear in the CFO's voice, the competitor installation nobody documented. You feed that back in. The system goes deeper. Each pass gets sharper because you're driving it. 30 diagnostic failure patterns. 22 frameworks. A verdict your board can act on: kill it, fix it, or accelerate it. The output is an Account Pack — but only as powerful as the operator behind it.
