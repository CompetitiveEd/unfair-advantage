Your problem isn't a lack of strategy. Your problem is the Execution Gap—the reality that brilliant boardroom strategies rarely survive contact with the buyer. Sales teams revert to pitching features and dropping prices out of fear.

1. The Automated Salesperson: If you allow an LLM to generate an email and send it directly to a CFO, you will destroy your credibility. GAIN strictly forbids automated outreach. AI processes the data; humans own the relationship.
