Competitor Intelligence

~15 MIN

You're not bidding in a vacuum. The evaluation panel is comparing you against specific alternatives.

W3-02 // COMPETITOR INTELLIGENCE SYNTHESIS
Act as a Competitive Intelligence Analyst.

Create a 'Competitive Positioning Matrix':
1. Their Likely Pitch: What will they claim is their USP?
2. Their Likely Ghosting: What will they whisper about us?
3. Our Counter-Narrative: How do we inoculate the client against their claims?
4. The 'Kill Shot': Where are they demonstrably weak where we are strong?

Scoring Strategy

~20 MIN

Not every requirement deserves equal effort. A criterion worth 25% of the total score deserves five times the writing investment.

W3-03 // TIER-BY-TIER SCORING STRATEGY

I am providing the Evaluation Criteria and Weightings.

Create a 'Scoring Strategy Table':
1. Rank criteria by 'Weighted Value'.
2. Define exactly what evidence/case studies we must include to score a 5/5.
3. Define the 'Minimum Viable Response' for low-value questions.

STEP 4 OF 7

Executive Summary

~30 MIN

The executive summary is the most important section. Most are about the bidder. This one is about the client.

W3-04 // EXECUTIVE SUMMARY GENERATOR
Act as a professional copywriter using the 'Made to Stick' framework.
Draft the structure of the Executive Summary.

Structure it in 4 Acts:
Act 1: The Context (The 'Why Now').
Act 2: The Complication (The 'Villain').
Act 3: The Resolution (The 'Hero'). NOT US. The Hero is the 'New Way of Working'.
Act 4: The Proof (Why Us).

Embed our 3 Win Themes (W3-01) throughout.

Self-Score

~20 MIN

Score your own response before they do. This prompt acts as an independent evaluator.

W3-05 // PRE-SUBMISSION SELF-SCORING
Act as a hostile procurement evaluator.
I am providing you with the Criteria and our Draft Response.

Task:
1. Score our response out of 5 based STRICTLY on the criteria. Be harsh.
2. Identify: "Where did we answer the question but fail to provide evidence?"
3. Provide 3 specific bullet points on how to move the score up by +1 point.
