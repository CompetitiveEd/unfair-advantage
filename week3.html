Most follow-ups are forgettable. They're either too generic ("Just checking in...") or too pushy ("Have you made a decision yet?"). Week 3 shows you how to use AI to create follow-ups that add value, demonstrate you were paying attention, and keep you top-of-mind without being annoying.

üí° The Outcome

Your follow-ups become conversations people look forward to, not emails they dread. You'll stay visible in the deal without burning goodwill, and you'll spot red flags early when momentum stalls.

Why This Matters

Deals don't die because someone said no. They die because they stalled out and got deprioritized. Strategic follow-up keeps you present and relevant without being annoying.

The difference between a rep who gets ghosted and one who stays top-of-mind often comes down to follow-up quality. Week 3 gives you the playbook.
