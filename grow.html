This is NURTURE — the fourth discipline of GAIN. Grow or die.
           
               
                    THE CONTEXT                     James had delivered brilliantly for 18 months. SLAs green across the board. Then he lost £2M of expansion business. Not because his delivery was poor. Because while he was focused on what he was already doing, someone else was having conversations about what the client needed next. [Nurture] Grow is the system that prevents this.                
           
           
                This week is different. This phase is different. Gather, Map, and Win are sequential. Grow is continuous. These prompts run monthly, quarterly, on every signal. They're the operating rhythm of account management.
