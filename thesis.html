WHY NOW

GAIN has always worked. For 25 years it was grunt work — paper copies, buried PDFs, Companies House filings pulled manually, conference programmes scanned for names. It worked. It just took weeks.

Four things changed:

SHIFT 01

THE INTELLIGENCE EXPLODED

Executives broadcast decisions on LinkedIn, YouTube, and conference stages. Regulator filings, contract data, Companies House records — all public. More actionable intelligence exists now than at any point in commercial history.

SHIFT 02

SO DID THE NOISE

75% of data is now AI-generated. The public web is flooded with synthetic content — agents writing reports, publishing analysis, generating databases autonomously. Without human verification, you're building strategy on someone else's hallucination.

SHIFT 03

REGULATION NOW DEMANDS IT

Financial services, utilities, public sector — regulated industries must now prove data lineage and provenance. The EU AI Act (August 2026) mandates human oversight for high-risk AI systems. Ofcom, Ofwat, FCA — all requiring audit trails. If your AI-assisted intelligence can't show where the data came from, it's a compliance liability, not a competitive advantage.

SHIFT 04

EQ+IQ IS THE ONLY FILTER

AI surfaces the intelligence. Only human judgment separates signal from noise. GAIN existed for 25 years as grunt work. Now it's the only methodology that combines AI speed with human verification — and the governance trail to prove it

The methodology hasn't changed. The landscape has. And without EQ+IQ, AI is just faster guessing.
