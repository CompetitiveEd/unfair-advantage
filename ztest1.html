PRE-MORTEM: START WITH THE END IN MIND RISK ANALYSIS

"Why will we lose?"

Most teams plan how to win. We start by understanding all of the reasons why we would lose. Then we assume we will win.

LOSE THEMES (EXAMPLE)
  • Perceived lack of US presence vs. Global Incumbent.
  • Failure to connect our culture to their "One Team" value.
  • Viewing us as a "Box Shifter" vs. Strategic Partner.
THE COUNTER-STRATEGY

We engage a third party to turn these specific fears into a positive Core Narrative. We don't hide the weakness; we reframe it as agility.

EXECUTION ROADMAP

How we move from chaos to system. The phased deployment model.

PHASE 1: PEOPLE

Restructure & Align

Restructure sales without impact/loss. Align bonus plans to goals. Benchmark pursuit roles and finalise package amendments.

PHASE 2: RELEVANCE

Customer Perspective

Answer "Why Us?" from customer perspective. Collate collateral. Understand sector information & marketing plans. Get all aspects of sales working as one.

PHASE 3: BRILLIANCE

Process & Governance

Clarify Strategic Pursuit Strategy. Launch market pricing. Utilize Ready Reckoner. Simplify bid production and governance approval process.

PHASE 4: WINNING

Market Dominance

Evidence new bid win rates increasing to >50%. Showcase Bid Pod success. Increased exploitation of upsell opportunity. Top Down approach in use.
