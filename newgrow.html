THE NARRATIVE

James delivered brilliantly for 18 months. SLAs green across the board. Then he lost £2M of expansion business. Not because his delivery was poor, but because while he was focused on what he was already doing, someone else was having a conversation about what the client needed next.

[Nurture] is the system that prevents you from being blindsided in your own account.

THE PSYCHOLOGY (MOMENTUM)

Accounts do not stand still. They are either growing or dying. The moment you transition from "Strategic Partner" to "Reliable Utility" in the client's mind, you have lost control of the account. You must manufacture momentum. The AI's job is to scan the horizon so you can focus on the relationship.

WHERE THIS BREAKS

FAILURE 1: The Silent Exit

The dashboard was green. The AI sentiment analysis said "Strong". But the key decision-maker stopped attending QBRs three months ago. We lost the renewal.

RULE: Data is not a relationship. If you stop reading the room, you lose.

FAILURE 2: The Fiction Campaign

Built a beautiful 90-day campaign. Reviewed it once. Never looked at it again. By the time the RFP arrived, the campaign was entirely fiction.

RULE: A campaign without weekly governance is just a document.

FAILURE 3: Delivery Entitlement

Delivery was excellent for four years. But when the renewal started, procurement had already shortlisted competitors. Someone else was selling while we were resting.

RULE: Delivery earns the right to renew. It doesn't guarantee it. Run the Threat Scan continuously.
