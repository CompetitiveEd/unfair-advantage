The Objective

Stop acting like a vendor who only calls at renewal time. The Nurture phase identifies silent friction, maps post-sale executive changes, and engineers a defensive moat 18 months in advance so renewals become unarguable non-events.

The Psychology

Buyers experience cognitive dissonance post-purchase. The incumbent is always at a disadvantage if they rest on their laurels. You must constantly re-align your deployed value to the board's evolving, moving risk mandates.

Data Governance Legend

Never feed internal client usage data, PII, or proprietary telemetry into public LLMs. Use aggregated, anonymised Service Delivery notes and updated public filings to track macro shifts securely.

Execution Ingredients

  • Original [G] Gather phase macro-radar.
  • Latest quarterly earnings transcripts.
  • Anonymised QBR/Service delivery summaries.
  • Updated executive movement alerts.

Human Checkpoint: OPSEC

Human Checkpoint: Context Override

Architecture / Purpose

Identifies how the board's mandate has shifted since the initial deal was signed, exposing gaps between deployed value and new priorities.

Engine Logic

Compares baseline assumptions against fresh data to explicitly find "Intelligence Decay"—what was true 6 months ago but is false now.

Power of "NONE"

If there is no explicit change in strategy or risk profile, the model must output "NONE". Prevents manufacturing fake urgency.

1. Any shifts in the CFO's core mandates or risk profile since the original deal.
2. New operational risks or headwinds introduced in this recent quarter.
3. How capital allocation or OPEX reduction targets have shifted.

Constraint: Base this strictly on the provided context. Do not invent shifts. If the strategy remains identical to the baseline, output exactly: "NONE".
