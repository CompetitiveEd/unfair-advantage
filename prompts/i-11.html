Architecture

Models what happens when specific people are in the same room at the same time. People behave differently in groups than one-to-one. The quiet influencer goes silent. The blocker performs for their boss. A private champion won't back you publicly because of who else is present. This engine takes A-14's relationship map and the confirmed attendee list and simulates the room dynamics — who dominates, who defers, who amplifies whom, who shuts down, where the energy shifts, and what you cannot say in front of Person X that you could say to them alone.

Engine Logic

IF A-14 + G-03 profiles + attendee list → full room simulation: dominance hierarchy, deference patterns, trigger topics per pair, safe/unsafe references, predicted question sources, attention map (who watches whom). IF A-04B available → layers evaluator-section routing onto room dynamics for bid presentations. Outputs: room behaviour model, speaker strategy (what to direct at whom), danger map (topics/names to avoid in this configuration), energy prediction (where attention will peak and drop), Q&A threat matrix.

Power of "NONE"

A-14 = None → no relationship data, models individuals in isolation (misses the whole point). G-03 = None → generic role-based assumptions, no psychological modelling. Attendee list = None → refuses to run. Cannot simulate a room without knowing who's in it.
