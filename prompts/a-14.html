Architecture

Two-layer engine. Layer 1 (static): relationship map — alliances, rivalries, dependencies, mentoring, gatekeeping, coalition opportunities, decision cascade paths, fault lines. Layer 2 (live — activates when Meeting Log provided): cross-meeting contamination risk (what you said to Person A that backfires with Person B), borrowed authority audit (whose name you can safely reference), narrative consistency check (have versions drifted across stakeholders?), meeting sequence intelligence (per-meeting contamination briefs with carry/bury/refs/versions). The key insight: a deal is one interconnected system, not separate conversations.
