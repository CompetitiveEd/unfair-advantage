Architecture

Living one-page Pursuit Plan + Dashboard. Orchestrates intelligence accumulation across every interaction. Calculates Pursuit Readiness in real time (9 weighted sections → 100-point score). Three modes: Generate/Refresh Full Plan, Meeting-Specific Brief, Post-Meeting Update. SALUTE field intelligence capture. Micro-Commitment Ladder tracking per stakeholder. Stakeholder Sentiment 1-5 with prescribed actions. Dashboard alerts (Stale, Single-Threaded, Low Readiness, Theme Decay). Auto-generates “Feed this to [prompt]” recommendations.

Engine Logic

Pursuit Readiness Score calculated on every output (80%+ = Full Conviction, 60-79% = Pursue with Gaps, 40-59% = Qualify Harder, <40% = Do Not Pursue). Post-meeting mode parses intel into SALUTE + explicit/implicit, updates all sections, recalculates scores, shows Intelligence Delta. Meeting Brief mode generates stakeholder-specific prep with Challenger insight and target micro-commitments.

Power of "NONE"

No GAIN outputs → Readiness Score <40%, shows priority order to reach 80%+. Missing sections degrade gracefully with prescribed actions.
