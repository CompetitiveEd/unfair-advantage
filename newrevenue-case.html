The Implementation Arc

01. Prove It (Day 1-30)

No abstract theory. We select one live, high-stakes deal or at-risk renewal. We run the GAIN architecture, map the friction, re-engineer the executive narrative, and win the deal. We earn the right to exist.

02. Codify It (Day 30-60)

Once the single deal methodology is proven, we extract the DNA of that win. We build a repeatable, AI-assisted workflow specific to your proposition and train a pilot pod of AEs to execute it independently.

03. Scale It (Day 60-90+)

The system rolls out to the wider portfolio. Intelligence gathering is compressed to 20 minutes per account. The narrative shifts universally from commodity vendor to strategic fiduciary. Margins multiply.

Where This Fails

Wrong Deal Selected

Trying to rescue a mathematically dead deal instead of a strategic pivot. If the competitor's software is already physically integrated into their core stack, strategy won't save you.

No Sponsor Authority

Deploying these tactics at the mid-management or user level. The GAIN methodology requires engaging the C-Suite and Economic Buyers; middle management will reject risk-based narratives.
