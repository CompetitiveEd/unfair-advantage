The Implementation Arc

01. Prove It (Day 1-30)

One live deal. High stakes, stalling or at risk. Map the hidden stakeholders. Rewrite the executive narrative. Close it. That's not training. That's licence to believe.

02. Codify It (Day 30-60)

The win becomes the playbook. AI-assisted intelligence, stakeholder mapping, narrative architecture — built for your proposition. A pilot pod runs the next deal independently. If they can't, the methodology failed.

03. Scale It (Day 60-90+)

Intelligence gathering drops from weeks to 20 minutes. Dead bids killed in week one, not quarter three. Cost of sale falls. Deal size rises.
