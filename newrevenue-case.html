The Objective

The Gather chain extracts board mandates, financial leaks, executive psychology, competitive positioning, and shadow hierarchies — then synthesises them into a battle card and a narrative lock that gets forwarded to the board. Six prompts. Each one a multi-phase engine with compound intelligence, routing logic, and forensic checklists.

The Psychology

Executives do not buy innovation; they buy the mitigation of operational friction. When you feed AI their exact earnings transcripts, you adopt their internal taxonomy. You stop sounding like a salesperson and start sounding like an operating partner who already understands their P&L.

Human Checkpoint: Data Integrity

Do not blindly copy/paste the output. Verify the numbers against the source PDF before briefing your team. The LLM is a synthesis engine, but you are the fiduciary of the account strategy.

Variables

Architecture

Engine Logic

Power of "NONE"
