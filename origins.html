The Landscape Shift

The Data That Didn't Exist

Before AI, stakeholder intelligence came from three sources: meetings, phone calls, and memory. You profiled the 5-10 people you could physically reach. Today, the landscape is radically different.

Today, a seller has access to:

LinkedIn

Every stakeholder's career history, posts, connections, endorsements, and professional identity, shared voluntarily.

Salesforce / CRM

Contact history, meeting notes, opportunity stages, pipeline data, service performance.

Companies House

Instant access to accounts, directors, filings.

Earnings Call Transcripts

Searchable, AI-parseable strategic narratives.

RFP Documents

Structured requirements revealing the political architecture of the decision.

Service Desk Data

Ticket volumes, SLA performance, user satisfaction patterns (if incumbent).

That's not a marginal improvement. That's a structural advantage.
