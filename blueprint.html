What Actually Happened

The diagnostic phase revealed a "Silent Exit" in progress: pipeline coverage was thin, and the sales team had no shared language for qualification. The resistance was real — senior sellers didn't want a framework. "I know my accounts" was the refrain.

Executive Insight for PE Partners

Methodology is not training; it is governance. Individual talent is a variable. A system is a constant. We build the operating rhythm that sustains performance after the 'hero' effort ends.

By month 18, the system proved itself. One account director used the mapping approach to find that his 'sure-thing' champion had been re-organised out. He changed strategy and won. He became the loudest advocate.
