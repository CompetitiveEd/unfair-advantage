These frameworks informed how the prompts are built. They're not a reading list — they're the structural logic underneath every GAIN output. Each week page tells you which framework applies and why it matters for that specific stage of the sales cycle.

No single framework wins a deal. The skill is knowing which one applies when — and that only comes from doing the work.
