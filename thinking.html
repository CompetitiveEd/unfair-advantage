AI is just a retrieval engine. Without a structural framework, it only retrieves noise. The prompts in the GAIN toolkit are strictly governed by these established principles of strategy, psychology, and executive alignment.

01. BEHAVIOURAL SCIENCE (DECODING THE BUYER)
[I] Influence

Loss Aversion

Kahneman & Tversky
The Core Logic

Humans feel the pain of a loss roughly twice as intensely as the joy of an equivalent gain. They default to the status quo to avoid perceived risk.

GAIN Application

Pitching "innovation" triggers risk pathways. Win themes must mathematically neutralise the fear of transition before promising a future gain.

[I] Influence

The Psychology of Persuasion

Robert Cialdini
The Core Logic

Decisions are accelerated by cognitive shortcuts: Social Proof, Authority, Scarcity, Liking, Consistency, and Reciprocity.

GAIN Application

AI scans public domains not just for capability, but for exact peer-industry matches to trigger the "Social Proof" shortcut in executive summaries.

[A] Assess

NLP Meta-Programs

James & Woodsmall / Bandler
The Core Logic

Every executive runs subconscious filters (e.g., Toward vs. Away From, Detail vs. Big Picture) that dictate how they process information and validate truth.

GAIN Application

Decoding stakeholder language patterns from LinkedIn posts/interviews to dynamically adapt the vocabulary and structure of the proposal.

02. STRATEGIC ARCHITECTURE (STRUCTURING THE DEAL)
[I] Influence

The Pyramid Principle

Barbara Minto
The Core Logic

Top-down communication. Start with the definitive answer, group the supporting arguments, and logically order the data beneath.

GAIN Application

Forces the "Answer First" rule. Executive summaries must deliver the EBITDA/ROI impact in the first 50 words. No chronological storytelling.

[I] Influence

Made to Stick

Heath & Heath
The Core Logic

For an idea to survive translation inside a complex organisation, it must be Simple, Unexpected, Concrete, Credible, Emotional, and wrapped in a Story.

GAIN Application

Arming internal champions. Passing the "Corridor Test" so your buyer can defend your premium price in 15 seconds to a cynical CFO.

[I] Influence

Blue Ocean Strategy

Kim & Mauborgne
The Core Logic

Do not compete in the existing market space (Red Ocean). Reconstruct market boundaries using the Eliminate-Reduce-Raise-Create grid.

GAIN Application

Shifting RFPs from spreadsheet price-comparisons to strategic leaps. Changing the buying criteria so incumbents become irrelevant.

[A] Assess / [I] Influence

The Case Method

Harvard Business School (TCS)
The Core Logic

Never guess. Find the precedent. When a complex deal stalls, the question is never "What should we do?" The question is "Where else has this exact friction occurred, what was the intervention, and what was the empirical result?"

GAIN Application

Replaces subjective sales theory with board-ready evidence. GAIN AI is prompted to retrieve specific, matched historical precedents rather than hallucinating creative solutions.

03. INTELLIGENCE MAPPING (NAVIGATING THE ROOM)
[A] Assess

Sphere of Influence

Sandhurst Military Intelligence
The Core Logic

The official chain of command rarely reflects how decisions are actually made on the ground. Power flows through informal networks and hidden alliances.

GAIN Application

The org chart is a map of titles. GAIN maps the informal influence to find the shadow decision-makers before the competitor does.

[G] Gather

Never Split the Difference

Chris Voss
The Core Logic

Tactical empathy. Use "Accusation Audits" to defuse negative dynamics and "Calibrated Questions" to give the illusion of control.

GAIN Application

Lowering buyer cortisol. Proactively listing the buyer's worst fears about your firm before they bring them up, clearing the path for logic.

[G] Gather

MEDDIC Qualification

Dick Dunkel / Jack Napoli
The Core Logic

A rigorous framework ensuring you don't chase unwinnable deals (Metrics, Economic Buyer, Decision Criteria/Process, Identify Pain, Champion).

GAIN Application

Used to kill dead pipeline early. AI analyzes the data gaps to highlight exactly which MEDDIC criteria are missing from your deal thesis.

[G] Gather / [I] Influence

Insight-Led Selling

Dr. Stephen Timme
The Core Logic

Stop speaking product; start speaking P&L. Executives do not buy technology; they buy financial outcomes. You must lead with financial intelligence and business acumen to defeat the status quo before ever mentioning a solution.

GAIN Application

Elevates the seller from IT procurement to the CFO's office. AI parses 10-K reports and earnings calls, mathematically mapping your technical capabilities directly to the buyer's EBITDA and working capital metrics.

04. EXECUTION DYNAMICS (THE OPERATING RHYTHM)
[N] Nurture

The Trust Equation

David Maister
The Core Logic

Trust is quantifiable: (Credibility + Reliability + Intimacy) divided by Self-Orientation. High self-orientation destroys trust instantly.

GAIN Application

Auditing outgoing communications. AI flags "we/our" dominant language to lower the Self-Orientation score and increase advisory intimacy.

[SYS] Governance

Human Amplification

Sol Rashidi
The Core Logic

AI must be deployed as a capability multiplier, not a replacement. Unsupervised AI creates digital decay; governed AI creates leverage.

GAIN Application

The structural premise of the site. AI handles the data parsing (minutes). The human handles the relational judgment (the win).

[I] Influence

Shipley Proposal Governance

Shipley Associates
The Core Logic

Winning complex bids requires a standardized, repeatable process built around early strategy lock-in, storyboarding, and rigorous color-team reviews.

GAIN Application

Prompting AI to act as a "Red Team" reviewer, scoring drafted sections against the bid's core win themes and compliance matrix before submission.

[N] Nurture / [SYS] Gov

Maverick (Radical Governance)

Ricardo Semler
The Core Logic

Trust scales; control does not. Give professionals the absolute truth, provide a rigid framework, and then get out of their way. Micromanagement is a tax on velocity.

GAIN Application

Eliminates middle-management bottlenecks and "compliance theatre." GAIN gives frontline sellers the intelligence to execute autonomously, replacing CRM nagging with high-velocity, outcome-based accountability.
