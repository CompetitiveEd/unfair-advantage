01. Authority & Influence 2x2

Generated from W2-05: Multi-Stakeholder Authority Map

The Authority & Influence 2x2 is the single most important visual in the methodology. It originated in 2005 as a hand-drawn chart with coloured stickers â€” one colour for awareness status (have you met them?), another for relationship quality (do they support you?). The original used four quadrants: Authority + Influence, Authority but No Influence, No Authority but Influence, No Authority and No Influence. Every person in the account was plotted. At a glance, you could see who matters, who likes you, and who you haven't reached yet.

This is the interactive version. Each stakeholder is plotted by their formal authority (vertical axis) and informal influence (horizontal axis). The colour coding shows relationship status â€” from unknown (blue) through targeted (amber) to established (green) and champion (white border).
