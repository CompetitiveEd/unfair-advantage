I-01 ▼

The Exec Summary Reframer

Rewrites the standard RFP intro into a narrative aligned with actual board-level friction, anchoring the entire submission around CEO/CFO pain points.

Variables

[RFP_EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY] [G_MACRO_RADAR]

Engine Logic

Strips generic vendor platitudes. Replaces "we are thrilled to respond" with hard risk mitigation metrics and financial certainty.

Power of "NONE"

If no board-level risk exists in the inputs, the engine must not invent one. It must output "NONE" to force a human review.

I-02 ▼

The Value Prop Aligner

Maps your generic product benefits strictly to the KPIs and stated objectives of the true Economic Buyer.

Variables

[OUR_STANDARD_VALUE_PROP] [A_ECONOMIC_BUYER]

Engine Logic

Ruthlessly deletes any feature that does not directly impact the buyer's stated bonus criteria, risk profile, or operational mandate.

Governance Constraint

Use exact metrics only. No generalized ROI claims. The AI must discard unprovable marketing numbers.

Pre-empts the internal objections from the specific hostile stakeholders identified during the Assess phase.

Engine Logic

Constructs specific 'landmines' for the incumbent/competitor and defensive moats for our own functional weaknesses.

Output Expected

A bulleted list of defensive statements ready to insert into the proposal methodology section.

Where This Breaks (Failure Modes)

Answering exactly what procurement asked, exactly how they asked it. Doing this effectively commoditises your solution instantly.

The Feature Dump

Using AI to effortlessly generate 10 pages of technical specifications the board will never read, burying your business case in noise.

The Ghost Writer

Relying on AI to write the entire bid end-to-end without SME injection. It sounds grammatically perfect, but says absolutely nothing of substance.
