WHERE THIS BREAKS

FAILURE 1: AI Homogenisation

Letting the AI write the final draft without a heavy human edit. The tone becomes "AI Corporate" (synergistic, robust, leading). The buyer immediately knows a machine wrote it.

RULE: AI drafts. Humans edit. Always read the Executive Summary out loud before sending.

FAILURE 2: The Compliance Trap

Answering the RFP questions exactly as written, without challenging the premise. You scored 100% on compliance, but lost because the incumbent wrote the questions.

RULE: Comply with the format. Disrupt the narrative.

FAILURE 3: The Feature Dump

You listed 50 technical capabilities. The buyer only cared about 3. The noise drowned out the signal.

RULE: If a feature doesn't map directly to a "Bleeding Neck" pain point, delete it from the Executive Summary.
