WHERE THIS BREAKS

Honest Failures

FAILURE 1: The Dashboard That Lied

Every RAG was green. SLA performance excellent. Then the client told us they were going to market. The dashboard measured delivery, not relevance.

RULE: Green SLAs don't mean green relationships. Measure "strategic alignment".

FAILURE 2: The Campaign Nobody Followed

Built a beautiful 90-day campaign. Reviewed it once. Never looked at it again. By week 4, the campaign was fiction.

RULE: A campaign without weekly governance is just a document.

FAILURE 3: The Qualification That Came Too Late

Pursued an opportunity for 3 months. Lost. Post-mortem revealed we should have walked away on day one. But nobody asked the hard questions until it was too late.

RULE: Qualify FIRST, not after investment.
