üõë OPSEC AIRWALL: SECURE YOUR DATA

Proprietary intelligence, Intellectual Property (IP), and customer-secured data must never be copied into a public, open LLM. If you are unsure, check your internal data governance policy. Do not feed internal CRM data, NDA-protected meeting notes, or internal product IP into public, open AI models. Switch to your secure, enterprise-approved AI environment before running any prompt marked with a Red 'Private Data' tag.

THE PROPRIETARY MULTIPLIER (GOOD TO GREAT)

Public data gets you to the door; proprietary data gets you inside. Before running these prompts, check your own CRM. Do you have notes from a past QBR? Did this executive use your product at their last company? Feed this hidden data into the prompts to build an insurmountable moat against generic AI outreach. Remember: if you put any proprietary data into any public prompts they become private and should be classified as private status.
